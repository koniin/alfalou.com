History

I started Alfalou to do some indie development on the side of my regular job. It started in 2022 but I have been on and off with game programming for a few years but it never ended up becoming something. Mostly because I am a programmer at heart which made me focus on the code and not the game.

Projects

Veil Runners / Steam page

Veil Runners is an action roguelite arena auto-shooter where you fight through waves of creatures to reach the next planet in search of the source. Level up, loot, buy randomly generated items and more to upgrade your character and units into an awesome and game-breaking build.
